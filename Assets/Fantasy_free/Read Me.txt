This is a fantasy-themed custom character.

 The package includes PSD files and exported PNG images. 

You can create various characters with freely combinable elements, and they can be used for expressions and lip sync animations depending on your needs.

It's also compatible with the paid version of the same series, so please feel free to give it a try.
